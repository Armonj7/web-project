Vet to Tech: The Journey of My Life

Hi! I'm Armon Jackson, I am originally from Florida and I moved to Washington state.
I attended Saint Martin's University for a highly rated program named Washington Vet 2 Tech.

Bio

I have always taken an interest in Technology but I never knew about how vast it was or what it is that I would even do. Over the years, after I separated from the military, I have had the opportunity to work on web development projects while attending a Tech program and its something I never knew that I'd enjoy doing.

My journey has been marked by obstacles, twists and turns but nothing happens by coincidence and that has been true for me throughout my life! Everything decision that I made has lead me where I am today and I wouldn't change it for the world.
