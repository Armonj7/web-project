About Me

I spent 5 years in the Air Force working as an aircraft mechanic. Since I left the Air Force, I took some time to discover myself and what I wanted to do as a recently separated military member. I decided become a real estate agent but I found that, I needed something different and frankly, with better job security.

Little did I know that after moving to washington State that there was a Technology program for veterans. I attended cohort for cybersecurity and after awhile I saw how difficult the job market was to start in that field as entry level. After much consideration with an opportunity to learn more, I attended another cohort. Ever since I learned about web development and how to build websites, I fell in love with the process. Although it is not easy but I cannot see myself loving anything else as much as I do with this field!
